Sedan Concept
Oil on Canvas, 16" x 20"
This sedan design is my interpretation of a modern standard sedan. I updated traditional styling by adding LED headlight strips that trail as far back as the center of the hood, wider wheel arches and bulging trim. This was also my first oil painting and I encountered both challenges and successes using this medium. The slow drying time if the oil paint meant I applied heavier layers to achieve desired colors and highlights. I used the oil paintsâ€™ excellent blending properties to create smooth areas of value in the mountains and road. I researched contemporary and vintage car advertisements as inspiration for a setting that would complement my design.
